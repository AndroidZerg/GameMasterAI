# Recommendations for Custom Photography

The following drinks had limited or no exact matches on free stock photo sites. A short in-house photo shoot is strongly recommended:

1. **Chocolate Chip Matcha Frappe** — No stock photos show green matcha with visible chocolate chips
2. **Matcha Milk Strawberry** — The layered green + pink look is too niche for stock sites
3. **Strawberry Matcha Frappe** — Blended pink-green combination not commonly photographed
4. **Strawberry Tajin Fruit Tea** — Chili-lime rimmed fruit tea doesn't exist in stock libraries

For these 4 drinks, the stock options above can serve as placeholders until custom photos are taken.
