   GAME RULES

  It is a time of enlightenment. From the Dark
Ages emerge four kingdoms, stumbling about

    and blinking their eyes, blinded by their
  newfound prosperity. Each kingdom seeks

        greatness. Who will achieve it?

   Two kingdoms are ruled by puny mortals
caught between their desire for power and their
need to please the Church. One kingdom is ruled

    by a secret cultist who has already fallen
      to temptation. The fourth kingdom
              is ruled by the devil.

 To win, you need resources. To win, the devil
           needs a piece of your soul.

    Is it time to make a deal?
