                                                          Owned Lots Pay

                                                            Each lot with a player marker (i.e., owned lot) pays
                                                          that player $1, no matter what card is drawn.

                                                          Casinos Pay

                                                            Each turn, the development company specified by
                                                          the card drawn pays money and points.

                                                            Most cards specify payment by all casinos matching
                                                          a single color, but 4 cards (including the End of Game
                                                          card) "Pay The Strip."
