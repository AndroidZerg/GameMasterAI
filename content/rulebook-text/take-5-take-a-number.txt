          Not a game for bullheads

Players:  2 - 10

Age:      10+ years

Playing time: approx. 45 minutes

Contents: 104 cards, 1 rules booklet

  IDEA OF THE GAME

The aim of the game is not to get any cards. Each card you
have to pick up will cost you a minus point per bullhead
shown on it. The player who has the fewest bullheads at
the end wins the game.

  PREPARATION

You will need paper and a pencil for scorekeeping. Shuffle the
cards and deal 10 cards to each player. Each player takes his
or her cards up in hand and arranges them according to their
numbers, in ascending order.
