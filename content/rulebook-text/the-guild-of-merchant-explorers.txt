   About the Maps

   1 There are 4 types of terrain: grassland ,
         desert , mountain , and sea .

   2 The capital space on each map is where you explore from at
         the start of the game. You cannot explore the capital space.

   3 A contiguous group of spaces of the same terrain type form                    2
         a region.

   4 There are 5 main types of spaces:                                    1

   Blank spaces.                                                                                             3
   You can discover new villages on these spaces when you have                        4
   fully explored a region. Villages earn you as shown in the
   score area on the map.

   Coin spaces.

   These earn you according to the number of coins shown.              5

   Ruin spaces.

   These earn you treasure cards.

   City spaces with values (2, 3, 4, or 5).
   These earn you when 2 cities are connected by your
   explorers, villages, and/or your capital space.

   Discovery spaces.
   These earn you as shown in the score area on the map.

   Some maps have additional types of spaces and terrain,
   which are detailed in the Explorer's Almanac.

   5 The score area at the bottom of the map shows you the value
         of discovered villages and discovery towers.

this way until the Exploration Deck is empty, marking the end of the era.
