You are farmers raising sheep, pigs, cows and horses. Three                            2 farm boards (one for each player)
workers help you keep the animals on your farm. They build                             4 farm expansions
stalls, enclose pastures and upgrade your farm. Stalls get                             4 "stall" tiles (with "stables" on the reverse sides)
expanded into stables and your cottage becomes a Half-                                 4 special buildings ("Half-Timbered House", "Storage Building",
Timbered House.                                                                           "Shelter" and "Open Stables")
                                                                                       9 different goods tiles (3x "4 sheep"; 1x "4 stone", "4 reed",
Each round, your workers carry out one action each. All of the                            "4 wood", "4 pigs", "4 cows" and "4 horses")
possible actions are clearly summarized on the game board. The                         1 start player tile
catch is that each action can only be used once per round. Since
you take turns placing your workers, you need the right timing                       wooden pieces
to be able to carry the actions most important to you at the right                   2 x 3 workers in the player colors (blue, red)
moment.
                                                                                       26 borders (yellow)
The winner is the player who has raised the most animals and                           22 sheep, 16 pigs, 13 cows and 14 horses
built the most valuable buildings at the end of the game.                              17 wood, 15 stones and 5 reeds
