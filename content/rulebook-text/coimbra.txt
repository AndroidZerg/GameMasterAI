                                         Playing the game

Coimbra is played over 4 rounds. Each round comprises the following phases:

A  Rolling dice                                       The first player creates the dice pool by rolling the dice.

B  Drafting and placing dice                          In player order, players choose one die each and place it onto a city location until each player has placed 3 dice.

C  Retrieving dice                                    In an order determined by the position of their dice, the players retrieve their dice from the city locations to gain
                                                      favor tiles and acquire character cards.

D  New player order                                   The new player order is determined by the number of crowns each player has.

E  Influence income                                   In player order, the players receive their influence income according to the color of their chosen dice.

F  Invest in a voyage                                 In player order, each player can invest in one voyage.

Afterwards, the new round is set up and 12 new character cards are put in the character display.

The game ends after the fourth round (when the character stack has run out). Then there is a final scoring (see pages 10 and 11).

The player with the most points wins the game.                                                                                                            22
