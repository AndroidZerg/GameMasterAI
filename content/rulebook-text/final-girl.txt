   The game is played in turns, each consisting of five phases. You will play until either you or the Killer are dead!  Most Action cards (and some other cards) require a Horror Roll. The number of dice you will roll is based on the
                                                                                                                        current Horror Level as depicted by the location of the Horror marker on the Horror Track.
   The Game Turn
                                                                                                                                 Each result of a 5 or 6 is considered a success.
   A turn in Final Girl has 5 phases:
                                                                                                                                 Each result of a 3 or 4 is considered a partial success. You MAY discard 2 Action cards from your hand
   1. The Action phase                                                                                                           to convert it into a success. You may do this once for each die showing a 3 or 4.
   2. The Planning phase
   3. The Killer phase                                                                                                  If none of the dice result in or are converted to a success, the Horror Roll has been failed.
   4. The Panic phase
   5. The Upkeep phase                                                                                                  The Horror Level is currently at 4. When you need to make a Horror Roll, roll two dice...

    Game Ambiguity

    Final Girl is a game system that is massive in scope, with card interactions and combinations that might as well
    be considered infinite in number. As such, it can happen from time to time that something may not be covered
    in the rules, there is a timing question, strange card interaction, a tie that cannot be adequately resolved, or
    something else that causes you uncertainty when playing. In these cases, apply one of the following rules:

    * The Rule of Infinite Evil - resolve it in such a way that results in the worst outcome for you.
    * The Rule of Abject Realism - resolve it in the way that results in the most realistic or thematic outcome.
    * The Rule of Never-ending Hilarity - resolve it in the way that results in the funniest outcome.

    We recommend choosing ONE of these rules before you play and resolving any ambiguities in that manner.
