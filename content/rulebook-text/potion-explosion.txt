# Game: Potion Explosion
# Publisher: Horrible Guild
# Players: 2-4 | Time: 30-45 min | Weight: 1.8/5

## OVERVIEW
Potion Explosion is a marble-drafting game where players are students at a magical academy trying to brew potions by collecting colored ingredient marbles from a shared dispenser. The core mechanic is a physical marble run — when you pick a marble and the marbles above it slide down, matching colors that collide cause chain-reaction "explosions" that let you collect even more marbles.

## COMPONENTS
- 1 Cardboard marble dispenser (with 5 slide tracks)
- 80 ingredient marbles in 4 colors (red/Unicorn Tears, blue/Ogre Mucus, yellow/Dragon Smoke, black/Fairy Dandruff)
- Potion tiles (8 types of potions, 2 of each per player count)
- Skill tokens (worth 4 points each for completing 3 potions of the same type or 5 different types)
- Little Help tokens (worth -2 points each)
- Score track / first player marker

## SETUP
1. Assemble the marble dispenser.
2. Randomly pour all 80 marbles into the dispenser so they fill the 5 tracks.
3. Shuffle potion tiles by type. Create stacks of each type. The number of stacks available depends on player count (5 stacks for 2 players, 6 for 3, all 8 for 4).
4. Place Skill tokens in a supply near the dispenser.
5. Each player takes 2 potion tiles from different stacks (their starting potions to brew).
6. Determine a first player.

## GAMEPLAY / TURN STRUCTURE
On your turn, you perform these steps in order:

### 1. Pick an Ingredient Marble
- Choose any single marble visible in any of the 5 tracks of the dispenser and remove it.
- After removal, marbles above slide down to fill the gap.
- **Explosion**: If the removal causes two or more marbles of the SAME color to collide (become adjacent), you collect ALL connected marbles of that matching color. This can trigger further chain reactions if subsequent collisions occur.
- You may only take ONE marble as your initial pick. Explosions are bonus.

### 2. Place Marbles
- Place collected marbles into the ingredient holes on your potion tiles. Each potion has specific colored holes that must be filled with the matching color marble.
- Any marbles you cannot or choose not to place go into your personal pool (limited to 3 marbles in your pool at any time). Excess marbles beyond 3 are returned to the dispenser.

### 3. Complete Potions
- If all holes on a potion tile are filled, the potion is complete. Flip it face down to your completed area.
- You may immediately drink a completed potion to use its power (see Potion Powers below), or save it for later.
- Draw a new potion tile from any available stack to replace the completed one.
- **Skill Tokens**: If you have completed 3 potions of the same type OR 5 potions of all different types, take a Skill token (4 points). Each set can only be claimed once.

### 4. Ask for a Little Help (Optional, any time on your turn)
- Once per turn, you may take 1 marble of your choice from any track WITHOUT triggering explosions. Take a "Little Help" token (-2 points).

### Potion Powers (used by drinking a completed potion)
You may drink completed potions on your turn (multiple allowed). Each type has a unique power:
- **Potion of Wisdom (blue/blue)**: Take up to 2 marbles from the dispenser (no explosions).
- **Potion of Magnetical Attraction**: Take a marble and all adjacent marbles of different colors from a track.
- **Potion of Prismatic Joy**: Use collected marbles as any color this turn.
- **Potion of Blind Faith**: Take 1 marble from the dispenser without looking (random from inside a track).
- **Potion of Clone**: Copy the effect of another potion you've already drunk this turn.
- **Abyssal Draft (black)**: Place up to 1 marble from your pool onto a potion; take all marbles of 1 color from another player's pool.
- **Sands of Time**: Re-use a potion you already drank this turn.
- **Filter of Lavamancing**: Discard up to 5 marbles from your pool and take that many from the dispenser (no explosions).

## SCORING / WINNING
- The game ends when a certain number of Skill tokens have been claimed (depends on player count: e.g., for 2 players the game ends after 4 Skill tokens taken; 3 players = 5; 4 players = 6).
- Finish the current round so all players have equal turns.
- **Scoring**: Each completed potion is worth the victory points printed on it (typically 2-5 points). Add 4 points per Skill token. Subtract 2 points per Little Help token.
- Highest score wins. Ties broken by most Skill tokens, then most completed potions.

## KEY RULES & TIPS
- **Explosions only happen on your pick** — marbles colliding during other effects (potion powers, Little Help) do NOT trigger explosions.
- **Pool limit is 3 marbles** — excess must go back in the dispenser. Managing your pool is important.
- **You can drink potions at any time during your turn**, including between other actions. Timing potion use well is key to big combos.
- **Plan for chain reactions** — the best turns come from spotting a marble whose removal will cause a cascade of 2-3 explosions.
- **Skill tokens are powerful** — 4 free points each. Aim for either 3-of-a-kind or 5-different early.
- **Little Help tokens are expensive** — -2 points each adds up. Use sparingly, ideally only when it lets you complete a high-value potion.
- **Potion of Prismatic Joy is very strong** — wildcard colors let you fill any potion holes. Prioritize it.
- **Watch the end-game trigger** — count Skill tokens taken. Don't get caught with half-finished potions.
