TELESTRATIONS - Official Rulebook (6 Player Family Pack)

OBJECT: To laugh and have fun. Draw what you see, then guess what you saw.

CONTENTS: 72 "This Side" and "That Side" Cards (over 800 words), 6 Erasable Sketch Books, 6 Dry-Erase Markers, 3 Clean-Up Cloths, 1 Die. 4-6 players.

SETUP: Place cards, cloths and die in center. Decide as group to use "THIS SIDE" or "THAT SIDE" of cards. Each player takes sketch book, marker, and card. Write name on front of book.

STEP 1 - GETTING A WORD: Roll die. Match number to word on card. Secretly write it in book. If blank space matches number, make up any word. If underlined category, make up word in that category.

STEP 2 - NUMBER OF PLAYERS: 4 or 6 players: Turn to SKETCH IT page 1, draw your word. 5 players: Turn to SKETCH IT page 1 then PASS to player on left (they look at word, then draw it). This ensures round ends on a guess.

STEP 3 - SKETCHIN: Everyone draws the Secret Word on SKETCH IT page 1. Take about a minute. When done, turn to page 2 to hide sketch and pass open book to player on left.

STEP 4 - GUESSIN: On GUESS IT page 2, flip back to see previous sketch, then write guess on page 2. Flip forward and pass to left.

STEP 5 - ROUND AND ROUND: Continue sketching, passing, and guessing until books return to owners. Books should end with a guess.

STEP 6 - THE BIG REVEAL: Flip through books sharing sketches and guesses.

RULES: No letters or numbers in sketches. No blank pages. Always write a guess even if silly. Pass open books simultaneously.

SCORING: Game is 3 rounds. Most points wins.
FRIENDLY SCORING: 1 point to player with your favorite sketch. 1 point to player with your favorite guess. 1 point to yourself if last guess matches secret word.
COMPETITIVE SCORING: Guessers earn 1 point if guess matches secret word or previous guess. Sketchers earn 1 point if sketch helps guesser match. 1 point to yourself if last guess matches secret word.

VARIANTS: Switch seats or reverse passing direction. Sketch with less dominant hand. Create custom cards. Mixed ages: youngest picks any word.

Publisher: USAOPOLY (The Op). 4-6 players.
