SKULL - Official Rulebook

CONTENTS: 6 tribes. Each tribe has: 3 flower-discs, 1 skull-disc, 1 card-playing mat. 3-6 players.

OBJECT: Succeed at two challenges. To succeed at a challenge, you must flip the corresponding number of discs without revealing a Skull. The 4 discs are also your 4 lives.

SETUP: Each player chooses 4 discs and play mat of a tribe. Place mat skull-side visible. Hold discs hidden. Choose first player.

STEP 1 - TURN PREPARATION: Each player chooses one disc, places it face-down on their mat. Then move to step 2.

STEP 2 - ADDING DISCS OR CHALLENGE:
ADDING: First player can add an extra disc on top face-down. Play continues clockwise. Players add one disc at a time, offset to show count. If no discs in hand, must issue challenge. Cannot challenge zero or more than total discs on table.
CHALLENGE: Player announces number of discs they intend to reveal from all mats. Clockwise, each player must either increase the bid or pass (slide mat to center, out of bidding). Last remaining bidder is the Challenger. Challenge includes all discs on all mats including passed players. Can overbid by more than one. Can directly bid maximum.

STEP 3 - THE ATTEMPT: Challenger flips discs equal to their bid. Must flip ALL of their own discs first. Then flip other players' discs in any order chosen. Flip one by one, top disc first. Don't have to flip all of an opponent's discs. If bid equals total discs, flip all one by one, stop at first Skull. ONE SKULL = failed. NO SKULLS = succeeded.

FAILED ATTEMPT: Stop immediately. All players return discs to hand. Challenger loses a disc permanently: shuffle remaining discs face-down, player whose Skull was flipped chooses which disc to eliminate (removed without revealing). If Challenger flipped own Skull, they choose which disc to lose. Lose last disc = eliminated. If eliminated, next first player is whoever's Skull was flipped. If own Skull, Challenger picks next first player. Cannot flip remaining discs after revealing Skull.

SUCCESSFUL ATTEMPT: Flip mat to flower-side up. If already flower-side up, you WIN THE GAME.

NEW ROUND: Challenger is first player of next round, resuming at step 1.

SPECIAL CASE - ONE DISC LEFT: During adding phase, must call challenge. During bidding, can pass or increase. If disc is revealed as Skull during challenge, eliminated.

VICTORY: Win a second challenge OR be the only player not eliminated.

GAME TIPS: Being first player is advantageous. Bluff by challenging even after playing Skull. Destabilize opponents with commentary. Beat opponents psychologically. Avoid 3-player games for beginners (highly tactical). Variant: combine 2 boxes for 7-10 players. Variant: 2 tribes per player.

Publisher: Space Cowboys. Designer: Herve Marly. 3-6 players.
